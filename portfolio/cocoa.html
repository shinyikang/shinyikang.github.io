Cocoa, the Farmers of Chocolate

Game for Social Change


Website | Weekly Documentation | GitHub

“Cocoa, the Farmers of Chocolate” is a physical-digital board game that raises awareness about the dark side of chocolate’s supply chain system. In the game, players become smallholder cocoa farmers in West Africa and are tasked with cultivating the plantation, selling cocoa beans, and overcoming challenges that cocoa farmers commonly face in the real world. During the gameplay, players get to experience, read, and talk about poverty, child labor, and deforestation associated with the cocoa industry.

“Cocoa” is an Interactive Media capstone project undertaken by Shinyi (Amy) Kang at New York University Abu Dhabi.
